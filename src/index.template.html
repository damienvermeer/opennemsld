Help & Information

This is an interactive Single Line Diagram (SLD) of the National Electricity Market (NEM).

  • Pan: Click and drag to move around the map.
  • Zoom: Use the scroll wheel or the +/- buttons in the top right.
  • Search: Use the search bar (Ctrl+F) to find substations by name.
  • Substation Info: Click on a substation name to zoom to it.
  • Generator Info: Hover over green circles to see generator details.

For more information, visit the AEMO website.
